## Highlights This Month

### Breakers Cup 2025 Kicks Off

The annual Breakers Cup tournament series has begun, with qualifiers running across major cities in Australia and the United States. This year's format introduces a new double-elimination bracket for the amateur division.

### Cue Tip Technology Advances

Japanese manufacturer Kamui has announced a new line of multi-layered tips designed for intermediate players. The tips feature a proprietary compression process that promises more consistent hits across a wider sweet spot.

### Women's Pool Growing Fast

The Women's Professional Billiard Alliance (WPBA) reported a 30% increase in tournament entries compared to last year. New sponsorship deals with major sports brands are helping to elevate the profile of women's cue sports globally.

### Australian 8-Ball Championships

The Australian 8-Ball Championships are scheduled for March 2025 in Melbourne. Early registrations suggest record participation numbers, with players from 6 countries confirmed.
