## Billiards Bulletin: Seo Crowned World Champion, WPA Celebrates Landmark Year, and Snooker Legends Shine!

What a whirlwind of action the world of billiards has seen recently! From nail-biting world championships to triumphant comebacks and a governing body celebrating a banner year, there's no shortage of excitement for fans across all disciplines. Let's dive into the latest news from around the world of cue sports.

## Seoa Seo Secures Historic Women's 9-Ball World Championship Title

The cue sports world watched in awe as South Korea's **Seoa Seo** etched her name into history, claiming the coveted Kamui WPA Women's 9-Ball World Championship title. Her victory wasn't just a win; it was a story of redemption, culminating in an epic rematch that had fans on the edge of their seats.

The World Pool-Billiard Association (WPA) highlighted her triumph as a fitting conclusion to a "landmark season" for the Predator Pro Billiard Series. This championship underscored Seo's undeniable skill and mental fortitude, solidifying her status as a global force in women's pool.

## WPA Reflects on a Remarkable 2025, Eyes Brighter 2026

Beyond individual triumphs, the broader landscape of pool is thriving, according to the World Pool-Billiard Association (WPA). The organization recently issued a statement celebrating a "remarkable 2025," citing successful tournaments, increased participation, and growing global interest.

This reflection isn't just a look back; it's a springboard for an "even brighter 2026," promising continued innovation and expansion within the sport. The WPA's proactive approach in fostering growth and supporting events like the Predator Pro Billiard Series ensures that pool remains a dynamic and engaging sport for both players and spectators worldwide.

## Joe Perry Delights in Derby, Crowned British Seniors Snooker Open Champion

Meanwhile, on the green baize of snooker, veteran sensation Joe Perry brought the house down in Derby, clinching the 2025 JenningsBet British Seniors Snooker Open title. Perry's performance was a masterclass in experience and precision, showcasing that the legends of the game still possess the magic to thrill.

This victory adds another prestigious trophy to his impressive cabinet and keeps the seniors' circuit vibrant and competitive. Fans of snooker also witnessed Chris Wakelin crowned champion in Edinburgh, further highlighting the ongoing excitement and high-stakes competition within the sport.
